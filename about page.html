ABOUT US

Founded in 2023, TravulSphere Transport Ltd. is an evolution of Genesys Project. A Household name in the transportation industry and one of the largest provider of intercity and interstate transportation,serving more than 200 destinations across Nigeria and West Africa with a modern, environmentally friendly fleet. The company has become an icon in the transportation industry, providing safe,enjoyable and affordable to millions of passengers annually.

Our Vision

A dynamic service organisation that provides timely transportation and logistics solutions with the highest level of excellence and customer satisfaction, as well as exceptional safety comfort, and economic value to our customers and partners.

Our Mission

To be recognised amongst current and prospective customers as the model of excellence in the transportaion industry thriugh integration of innovatiove business practices and consistent emphasis on customer satisfaction.

Safety

Goal is to get everyone safely to their destination. Our drivers are civil, experienced, highly trained professionals . Our vehicles are inspected and regularly maintained to ensure reliabilty. our flexibilty allows us to meet the ever-changing needs of our customers.

Teamwork

Together Everyone Achieves more. Our employees conduct themselves with the highest level of honesty, trust and ethical standards. Our understanding that accountability to each other and our customers is vital for success.

Community

Community -Our past,present and future. our company is locally owned,locally staffed and locally operated. Our commitment to the communities we serve inspires company-wide philanthropy through donations and schlorships. Our pledge for growth will stem through ongoing education and opportunities.
